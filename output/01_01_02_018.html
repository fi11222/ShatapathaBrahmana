अथ देवताया आदिशति | सर्वा ह वै देवता अद्ह्वर्युं हविर्गृहीष्यन्तमुपतिष्ठन्ते मम नाम ग्रहीष्यति मम नाम ग्रहीष्यतीति ताब्ह्य एवैतत्सह सतीब्ह्योऽसमदं करोति

atha devatāyā ādiśati | sarvā ha vai devatā adhvaryuṃ havirgṛhīṣyantamupatiṣṭhante mama nāma grahīṣyati mama nāma grahīṣyatīti tābhya evaitatsaha satībhyo 'samadaṃ karoti

18. He now announces (the oblation) to the deity (for whom it is intended). For when the Adhvaryu is about to take the oblation, all the gods draw near to him, thinking, 'My name he will choose! my name he will choose!' and among them who are thus gathered together, he thereby establishes concord.
